A paragraph is a unit of writing focused on a single idea, consisting of one or more sentences that are typically organized around a topic sentence. Paragraphs are usually marked by an indentation of the first line or a skipped line, signaling a new section to the reader and helping to structure a longer piece of writing by organizing thoughts and topics.
